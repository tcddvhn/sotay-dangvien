# Cach noi voi Codex sau khi backup day du

Sau khi anh/chị da bo sung du file con thieu va manifest da duoc chot, co the dung 1 trong 2 cau:

```text
Khoi phuc lai he thong truoc day
```

hoac

```text
Khoi phuc lai he thong truoc day tu bo backup C:\Users\ldkie\OneDrive\Documents\GitHub\sotay-dangvien\backups\backup-truoc-phan-quyen-20260415-1030
```

Neu muon toi kiem tra truoc khi xem backup da san sang hay chua, nhan:

```text
Kiem tra bo backup hien tai da du rollback chua
```
